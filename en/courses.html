OTHM Professional Certificate

(NQF Level-4)

The OTHM Certificate is assessed by four three hour written examinations marked by the OTHM examiners. Satisfactory completion of the Certificate allows you to progress to the OTHM Diploma, which is the next stage in the OTHM qualifications track.

The module covers the following:

  • Introduction to Travel, Tourism and Hospitality
  • Introduction to the Business Environment
  • Introduction to Business Communication and Customer Care
  • Introduction to Business and Finance

OTHM Level 4 Professional Certificate Entry Requirements:

  • Holders of OTHM Introductory Certificate or
  • 5 GCSE's or 5 O'Levels or equivalent or
  • Equivalent academic qualifications or
  • Mature students with at least 5 years' relevant experience
  • English Language Competency (IELTS 5.5 or equivalent)

The OTHM Diploma is assessed by six three hour written examinations marked by the OTHM examiners. Satisfactory completion of the Diploma allows you to progress to the OTHM Higher Diploma, which is the next stage in the OTHM qualifications track.

The module covers the following:

  • Tourism Marketing and Management
  • Hospitality Marketing and Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Tourism and Hospitality Operations Management
  • Financial Management and Information Systems
  • Customer Relations and the Business Environment

OTHM Level 5 Professional Diploma Entry Requirements

  • Holders of OTHM Professional Certificate or
  • Equivalent academic qualifications or
  • Mature students with a minimum of 8 years' relevant experience including 3 years' management experience
  • English Language Competency (IELTS 5.5 or equivalent)
