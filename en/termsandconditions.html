  1. Student Warranties

    ISTHI reserves the right to disclose student’s details, including academic progress and attendance rates to the Home Office.

    2. Should the student pay a deposit of 50%, they are required to pay the remaining 50% of the first course fee by the induction date, and the remaining fees for the 2nd year must be paid within the next 6 months of the last payment.
    3. Students who have not paid on time will not be granted admission on to ISTHI.

    5. A charge of £150.00 will be made for all dishonoured payments.

    7. Students may defer their place on a programme by up to one academic year subject to awarding body regulations.
    8. Non-payment of fees – ISTHI reserves the right to suspend any dismiss any student, at any time, for non-payment of fees or breach of student contract. No fees will be refunded for any student dismissed under this clause.

    2. Students will be expelled from the programme and will not be entitled to any if they (i) do not adhere to the attendance requirements; (ii) fail to start the course as scheduled without prior notification to ISTHI.
