Introducing ourselves

Why ISTHM?

International School of Tourism and Hotel Management is a dynamic international School based in Toledo, Spain. Our philosophy is to provide a stimulating learning environment in which our students can develop their potential and successfully achieve academic, professional and personal goals.

We firmly believe that further education should be accessible to all students regardless of background. Hence, we strive to offer competitively priced courses, focusing our resources on maintaining high teaching standards and comprehensive student care.

The review team has confidence in ISTHM's management of its responsibilities for the standards of the awards it offers on behalf of its awarding organisations.

The review team has confidence that ISTHM is fulfilling its responsibilities for managing and enhancing the quality of the intended learning opportunities it provides for students.
