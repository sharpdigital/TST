ITST - OTHM

ITST is proud of representing such a respectful and influential institution in the Travel, Tourism and Hospitality industry. We could not trust anyone else to give our students the best qualifications and the certainty of a successful career.

OTHM is a membership body founded in London in 2001 by a group of specialists from the industry and academia. OTHM was incorporated in 2003 as a not for profit company.

The aim of OTHM is to support the people who work in the tourism and hospitality industry. In our Mission Statement we state that OTHM:

  • Aims to act as a forum for the discussion of issues relevant to the industry
  • Promotes professionalism in the industry
  • Promotes ethical practice and environmental awareness in the industry


Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management (QCF)

OTHM Curriculum provides students with a sound understanding of the Travel, Tourism and Hospitality field and skills essential for successful employment in the Industry. OTHM provided all the resources in terms of their professionally prepared lecture notes and guidance. Once you complete the OTHM qualification you will feel confident and prepared to excel in Tourism and Hospitality Industry. Courses and qualifications we provided in this area are:

  1. OTHM Level 4 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management
  2. OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management
  3. OTHM Level 6 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management

Duration: 2 years

Progression: Final Year BA (Hons) in Tourism and Hospitality at British Universities, allowing holders of the OTHM Level 6 Diploma to have direct entry to the final year BA (Hons) in Tourism and Hospitality.
