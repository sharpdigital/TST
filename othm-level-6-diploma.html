OTHM Level 6 Diploma

OTHM Level 6 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF)

Who is this suitable for?

OTHM Level 6 Diploma In Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF) is suitable for people who work in the Tourism and Hospitality Industry and who have already gained an OTHM Level 5 or other qualifications at a suitable level. This qualification is also appropriate for middle and senior managers.

Work experience

OTHM Level 6 Diploma students are expected to either have work experience in the industry before joining the course or be working part-time in the industry during the course or both. Candidates should note that if they intend to study for further professional qualifications in the industry a certain amount of industry experience will probably be required.

Units (QCF)

OTHM Level 6 Diploma In Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF) consists of 6 Units:

  • Marketing Communications for Tourism and Hospitality with Work Placement
  • Working in the International Tourism and Hospitality Business Environment with Work Placement
  • Analysing Tourism and Hospitality Businesses with Work Placement
  • Using Management Information Systems for Tourism and Hospitality with Work Placement
  • Strategic Human Resources Management in Tourism and Hospitality with Work Placement
  • Researching Tourism and Hospitality Operations with Work Placement

Total Credit: 120

Overall Grading Type: Pass / Merit / Distinction

Assessment Methods: Written assignment

Entry requirements

  • Academic: OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management (QCF) or relevant UK Level 5 qualification or equivalent overseas qualification as recognised by UK NARIC
  • Work experience and age: Mature students (over 21) with five years relevant work experience, including three years with the team leadership or managerial experience
  • English: If you are not from the United Kingdom or a majority English-speaking country you must provide evidence that you are competent in English language at a minimum of CEFR level B2 from a recognised English test provider.
Progression

On completion of OTHM Level 6 Diploma In Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF) students will be considered for various courses in recognised colleges and universities in the UK and around the world or go on to study the OTHM Level 7 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management (QCF).

Key information
