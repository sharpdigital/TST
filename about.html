About us

Introducing ourselves

Why ITST?

It is a dynamic international School based in Toledo, Spain. Our philosophy is to provide a stimulating learning environment in which our students can develop their potential and successfully achieve academic, professional and personal goals.

We firmly believe that further education should be accessible to all students regardless of background. Hence, we strive to offer competitively priced courses, focusing our resources on maintaining high teaching standards and comprehensive student care.

International Tourism School Toledo committed teachers and administrative staffs work diligently to support the efforts of its students. In understanding the crucial role they play in the School’s daily successes, we are committed to recruiting the high quality individuals to join ITST’s diverse and dedicated team.
