OTHM Level 5 Diploma

OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF)

Who is this suitable for?

OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF) is suitable for people who have passed the OTHM Level 4 Diploma (or equivalent) or who are already working at supervisory or management level. On completion of the OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management, you can also become an Associate Member of OTHM.

Work experience

OTHM Level 5 Diploma students are expected to either have work experience in the industry before joining the course or be working part-time in the industry during the course, or both. Candidates should note that if they intend to study for further professional qualifications in the industry a certain amount of industrial experience will probably be required.

Units (QCF)

OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF) consists of 6 Units:

  • Managing Marketing Planning in Tourism and Hospitality with work placement
  • Managing Operations in Tourism and Hospitality with work placement
  • Managing Financial Accounts in Tourism and Hospitality with work placement
  • Managing Marketing Objectives in Tourism and Hospitality with work placement
  • Managing Human Resources in the Tourism and Hospitality with work placement
  • Managing Customer Relations in the Tourism and Hospitality with work placement

Total Credit: 45

Total GLH (Guided Learning Hour): 110

Overall Grading Type: Pass / Merit / Distinction

Assessment Methods: Written assignment

Entry requirements

  • Academic: A level standard or OTHM Level 4 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management (QCF) or relevant UK Level 4 qualification or equivalent overseas qualification as recognised by UK NARIC
  • Work experience and age: Mature students (over 21) with relevant work experience (ideally with team leadership or managerial experience)
  • English: If you are not from the United Kingdom or a majority English-speaking country you must provide evidence that you are competent in English language at a minimum of CEFR level B1 from a recognised English test provider.
  • Students from outside the European Economic Area (EEA) or Switzerland: More information here
Progression

On passing the OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF) student will be eligible to be considered for the OTHM Level 6 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management (QCF) or OTHM Level 6 Diploma in Tourism and Hospitality Management with Work Placement (QCF). Students must pass all Level 5 units to be eligible to join the OTHM Level 6 Diplomas.
