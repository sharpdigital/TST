Testimonials

Francesca Monaco

Lecturer
 

"The thriving tourism job market constantly needs skilled professionals. By means of this Diploma Lisa and I aim to prepare students who wish to pursue a career in this field by providing them with both practical and theoretical skills. As a lecturer my goal is for students to master the relevance of the contents of the units taught in class and guide them along throughout the course. I understand students may struggle at the beginning due to the very diverse nature of the British higher education as compared with the Spanish one, not to mention the use of the vehicular language itself: English. However, this challenge gives students an opportunity to foster self-confidence and express their ideas clearly".
