Progress & Pathways

Opportunities with OTHM

OTHM qualifications are registered with Ofqual/QCF (Office of the Qualifications and Examinations Regulation) and student applications for further studies are welcomed by many universities in the UK.

Final Year BA (Hons) in Tourism and Hospitality

OTHM has a key relationship with London South Bank University-LSBU allowing holders of the OTHM Level 6 Diploma to have direct entry to the final year BA (Hons) in Tourism and Hospitality.

Additional university progression routes

The following universities are also pleased to accept OTHM qualified students to forward applications for direct entry into year one, year two and final year of their BA (Hons) in Tourism & Hospitality or in relevant cases direct entry into Post Graduate and Masters level programmes at the university.

  • University of West London -formerly known as Thames Valley University
  • University of Salford
  • University of Hertfordshire
  • University of Huddersfield
